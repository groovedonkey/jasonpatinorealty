About Jason

I'm Jason Patino, a proud Floridian, REALTOR®, and Senior Director of Recruitment for the Military Veterans Team at LPT Realty, selling residential real estate, and managing vacation rentals since 2017 across Pinellas and Hillsborough Counties. Prior to my career in real estate, I ran recruiting departments for Fortune 100 and 500 companies. After joining the Military Veterans Team in 2023, I quickly transitioned into a full-time listing and buying agent, and in 2024, I earned Top Sales Person of the Year while leading recruitment and training for the team. My local expertise, sharpened by years running a successful vacation rental business, gives me a unique edge in identifying prime investment properties and dream homes alike. Whether you're relocating, searching for your ideal home, or exploring real estate as a career, I'm here to guide you with dedication, market insight, and a commitment to excellence. Let’s connect and turn your real estate goals into reality.
